How we work.

InstaDriver is a personal driver and chauffeur service that offers drivers on demand to take you wherever you want to go in the comfort of your own car.

We are focused on providing superior service and referring dependable drivers for hire, whether you need a driver a month, a day or just an hour.

From occasional travels to recurring medical appointments to a driver who operates your cooperate vehicle or shuttle, we've got you covered.

InstaDriver Drivers

Hiring Procedure

All candidates undergo an initial, in-person interview as well as a background check covering at three nationwide databases.

Our drivers undergo a 4 week training on driving,etiquette and first aid emergencies.

Our drivers are Lagos State Drivers Institute (LASDRI) certified.

The average age of our drivers is 35, and every single driver has at least 5 years of proffesional driving experience.

Insurance

What’s more, our drivers are dedicated to providing their clients with a luxury experience.

Your driver will arrive at your door – on time – in professional dress.

In addition to the driver’s personal insurance, he or she is covered by a $2 million insurance policy custom-made for this type of service.

So you can rest assured you’re well taken care of – and when you find a driver you like, you can request him or her again.
